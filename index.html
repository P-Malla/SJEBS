Welcome to SJEBS

Shishu Jyoti English Boarding School is one of the finest institutions in its provience as it is unique in its kind. SJES is founded by a group of highly qualified, educated and experienced professionals and is located in dhangadimai municipility of Siraha district. SJEBS has been serving since 20 glorious years in transforming education.

SJEBS aims to offer a challenging and balanced education. It is committed to society at large and aims to fulfill its obligations to the communities in touches with appropriate initiatives in a dedicated and comprehensive manner.

SJEBS feel proud to offer a caring family atmosphere in which er see the development of whole child academically, spritually and socially. We aim for our children to become responsible adults making a positive contribution to society and whole nation as well. Quality education being its top most priority the school provides ample opportunities to the students for their overall development. Today, the role fo school is not only to purcive academic excellence but also to motivate and empower its students to the life long learners, critical thinkers and productive members of an ever changing global socirty.

We believe that, the parents are the most strenghting power in moulding the future of children. Your consistent support empowers us to do more and more. Our children future is our primary concern and we look forward to working in partnership with you all. Hopefully our website will provide with useful information and enable you to gain insight into our school. However, a visit to the school is only way to really appreciate the uniqueness of SJEBS.
